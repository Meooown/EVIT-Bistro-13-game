Welcome to squid game. you are a food delivery agent delivering calimary from the BISTRO resturaunt to the Superintendent!

-Cops (with tasers) mistake your food to be a lethal weapon. Avoid at all costs.
-the red road and spikes are dangerous.
-land on bistro cuisine to replenish your energy!
-Green = Victory!'
